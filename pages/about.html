My Story

I am an American of Haitian-descent with a burning passion for making software. I have an older sister and a younger brother, and usually show them the projects I am working on. I started programming in middle school, fascinated by how people were able to create software that can work so well with hardware. I started making games and small programs in C++ and Visual Basic in high school, furthering my love for technology and coding.
I enjoy fishing, gardening, and web development, with having some experience in Bootstrap and React.
